This folder is for room photos. 

Add your room images here with these suggested names:
- main-view.jpg (main room view)
- cozy-corner.jpg (reading nook or seating area)
- bathroom.jpg (bathroom view)
- window-view.jpg (view from the window)
- bed-detail.jpg (close-up of the bed area)
- amenities.jpg (special amenities or details)

Images should be high quality (at least 1200px wide) and in JPG or PNG format.
